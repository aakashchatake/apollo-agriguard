Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a demo of Apollo AgriGuard?

Contact our sales team via the form above. We offer virtual demos, on-site consultations, and real-world field visits. Most demos can be scheduled within 48-72 hours.

What are the system requirements for deployment?

Apollo AgriGuard works with farms 5+ hectares. We require GPS connectivity, basic charging infrastructure, and a control center setup. Our team handles all installation and configuration.

How much does implementation cost?

Pricing varies based on farm size, automation level, and support requirements. Contact our team for a customized quote. We offer flexible financing and ROI guarantees.

What kind of ongoing support is available?

We provide 24/7 technical support, regular maintenance, software updates, and continuous optimization. Dedicated account managers ensure your success.
